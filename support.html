Frequently Asked

How do I restore my "Remove Ads" purchase?
Open Settings (the gear on the main menu) and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the original purchase.

My scores aren't showing on the leaderboard.
Sign in to Game Center in your device Settings, then reopen the app. Scores are submitted automatically at the end of each run.

How do I play?
Holds slide down the wall — tap each one before it falls off the bottom. Grab high on the screen for a PERFECT. Miss three holds and you fall.
